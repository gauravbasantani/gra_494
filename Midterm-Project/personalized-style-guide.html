India

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a revered Sikh shrine located in Amritsar, India. Adorned with golden domes and white marble, it stands in the middle of a sacred pool known as the Amrit Sarovar. The temple is a symbol of spiritual and cultural significance for the Sikh community, attracting millions of pilgrims and visitors each year. Its tranquil surroundings and the divine atmosphere make it a place of deep reverence and contemplation.
